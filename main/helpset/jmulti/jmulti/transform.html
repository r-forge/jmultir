Transforming Time Series

General Remark

The transform dialog can be accessed by a RIGHT mouse click over one or more selected time series in the time series list .

Differences

If differences of a selected series are taken then the start date of the newly created differenced series is adjusted automatically.

Suffixes

  • _d1 first differences: y(t) - y(t-1)
  • _d1_d1 second differences: [y(t) - y(t-1)] - [y(t-1) - y(t-2)]
  • _dseas seasonal differences: y(t) - y(t-freq)
  • _log logarithm (levels): ln(y(t))
  • _log_d1 1st differences of logarithms: ln(y(t)) - ln(y(t-1))
  • _log_d1_d1 2nd differences of logarithms: [ln(y(t)) - ln(y(t-1))] - [ln(y(t-1)) - ln(y(t-2))]
  • _log_dseas seasonal differences of logarithms: ln(y(t)) - ln(y(t-freq))
